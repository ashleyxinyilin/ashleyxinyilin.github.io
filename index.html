GROWTH ARCHITECT

"I don't just translate language — I translate how Chinese headquarters and European partners think, move, and decide."

Based in Düsseldorf for five years, I am a Senior Project & Account Manager at KleinS GmbH, a full-service market entry agency helping APAC brands establish and grow in Europe. My client portfolio spans EcoFlow, OPPO, Jackery, Anker, XGIMI, JD.com, Alibaba, Xpeng, Geek+, Aiko, AgiBot, MOVA, and China Mobile — across Germany, France, Benelux, Nordics, Eastern Europe, and beyond.

My work covers the full commercial cycle: identifying opportunities, qualifying prospects, negotiating and closing, executing on the ground, and building the long-term relationships that generate the next engagement. I have led brand launches, dealer conferences, retail activations, and trade show presences across more than 10 European markets.

What makes me effective in APAC-facing roles is cultural fluency, not just language fluency. Chinese HQ teams and European commercial partners operate on fundamentally different rhythms — I turn that gap into a coordination advantage that both sides rely on.
