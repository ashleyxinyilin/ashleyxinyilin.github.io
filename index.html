About Me

Cross-Border
Growth Architect

"Driven by curiosity, resilience, and a passion for solving interesting business challenges, I help companies navigate cultures, build partnerships, and uncover growth opportunities across borders."

Based in Düsseldorf for five years, I am a Senior Project & Account Manager at KleinS GmbH, a full-service market entry agency helping APAC brands establish and grow in Europe. My client portfolio spans EcoFlow, OPPO, Anker, XGIMI, JD.com, Alibaba, Xpeng, Geek+, Aiko, AgiBot, MOVA, and more — across Germany, France, Benelux, the Nordics, and Eastern Europe.

My work covers the full commercial cycle: identifying opportunities, qualifying prospects, negotiating and closing, executing on the ground, and building the long-term relationships that generate the next engagement. I have led brand launches, dealer conferences, retail activations, and trade show presences across more than 10 European markets.

What makes me effective in APAC-facing roles is cultural fluency, not just language fluency — I turn the gap between APAC headquarters priorities and European commercial expectations into a coordination advantage both sides rely on. Having worked across countries, industries, and teams, I've learned that the best results come from trust, adaptability, and strong collaboration. I stay curious, keep asking questions, and take pride in being the kind of teammate others can count on.
